Skills and jobs

WANT:

A UI designer

The core task of UI designer is to implement the screen based on the plan document and wire frame delivered from the plan. In this process, it is important to consider the overall look & feel by looking at the plan and wire frames, and to plan the components and overall screen composition accordingly. The most important part is excellent observation. And it is necessary to constantly grasp the design trend and make efforts. Also, in the case of UI designers, communication skills are very important because the work is done through collaboration with various designers

A Developer

The role of the developer includes building trust with other teams in the design and design designed to create the service. Developers, as developers, provide their reliable source stability and technical skills.
The first necessary ability is problem-solving ability. Developers also face numerous barriers. When you have a lot of problems, it's important how you can define them and find solutions. The second required ability is implementation. We need to code for complex real-world problems. You have to know for yourself how to abstract this reality and how to define the problem. The third necessary skill is learning ability. The trend in IT technology is fast. The technology that leads in the next five to ten years is changing rapidly. And the paradigm of technology is changing rapidly. Fourth, necessary skills are communication skills and teamwork. Conflicts can arise when developers collaborate. It is also an ability to resolve this conflict

A Software tester

The core task of software tester is to be familiar with and master the testing process, to be familiar with the basic operation of database, familiar with various interface testing tools. In this process, the first and most important thing is to have strong learning ability and executive ability, have strong logical reasoning ability to carry out preliminary software testing, in addition, software testers should be good at summarizing and thinking, to have a sense of responsibility, team spirit, can withstand high intensity of work pressure.

A Development manager

The development manager is the leader of the specific development process and manages the people under the project. First, the development manager must be an expert who is familiar with the business and development technology. The development manager's job is to define the requirements and determine the appropriate technical architecture and systems, of course, in the process. The most important thing is to ensure that the software product is developed according to the design standards.
